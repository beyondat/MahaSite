AI SNS Service

It is a service jointly developed with a start-up company located in Silicon Valley Mateo. When information available to users is available without having to order based on data obtained via e-mail, SNS or other devices, users are given a very interesting story and question based on their current activity, personality, and emotional analysis. In addition, through conversation with the user, the user can use the relevant service APIs, such as getting directions, registering a schedule, notifying a schedule, making reservations, conducting a health check, and searching for information at the same time. The AI ​​service obtained from this process and the user's question and answer data are also recorded and used to analyze the user's behavior pattern and personality type in the future. This user-related data collection has a self-learning AI model through cycles of puzzles, questions, and answers.
